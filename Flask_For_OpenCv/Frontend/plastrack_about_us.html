Our Collaboration

We are a dedicated team of developers from City of Mandaluyong Science High School who came together to create PlasTrack - a reward-based plastic bottle disposal system designed to encourage proper waste management and reduce plastic pollution in our community.

Through collaboration, innovation, and a shared passion for environmental sustainability, we've developed a system that makes recycling rewarding and accessible for everyone. Each team member brings unique expertise to create a comprehensive solution that addresses both technical and user experience challenges.
