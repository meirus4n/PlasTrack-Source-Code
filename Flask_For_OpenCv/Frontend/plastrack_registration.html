Data Privacy Notice

PlasTrack is committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

By registering, you acknowledge that:

  • We collect personal information necessary for your account and recycling tracking
  • Your name and username may be displayed publicly on the PlasTrack website
  • Your data will be processed securely and used only for the purposes stated
  • You have rights to access and correct your personal information
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
  • Sensitive data (like your password) is securely stored and never shared

Read the full Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) at: privacy.gov.ph/data-privacy-act/
