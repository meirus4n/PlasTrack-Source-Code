How to Use PlasTrack

Step 1: Log In

Access your PlasTrack account.

Step 2: Insert Bottle

Place an empty plastic bottle into the input slot of the machine.

Step 3: Enter PIN

Enter your User PIN to identify your account.

Step 4: Earn Points

Each bottle scanned earns you points instantly!

Step 5: Redeem Rewards

Trade your points for snacks, school supplies, or other cool items.

Step 6: Repeat & Go Green!

Keep recycling and make a positive impact on the environment.
